Defense and Development

ProDefesa is a multidisciplinary research group that develops a wide range of studies, from the analysis of the defense and aerospace industries to the observation of innovation and economic development in its varied nuances. The group relies on the collaboration of professors, researchers and graduate students and develops relationships with various national and international teaching and research institutions, seeking to collaborate with academic and technical studies in favor of industrial, economic and social development.
